Go Dukes!

After college, I worked as a full-stack Software Developer at CarMax in Richmond, VA. My team was tasked with rebuilding the proprietary appraisal software.

Some highlights from my time at CarMax:

  • Performed business analysis role and worked with stakeholders and product owner to write business requirements as needed.

  • Coordinated software update releases, managing communication and timing of several teams and stakeholders.
  • Participated in daytime and after-hours on-call support rotation in event of a systemic problem or outage.
  • Planned and participated in college recruiting events, community service team builder events, and department-wide employee engagement events.

Wanting to relocate from (my hometown of) Richmond, I joined ISL in December of 2016 and moved to DC. At ISL, I focus on front-end web development.
